My name is Emma Rodam and I study multimediadesign at UCL in Odense. My passion is code and graphic design and my ambition is to become a webdesigner after I graduate.
I love to be creative in any way - draw, paint, sew, play music ect., and I want my work to reflect that.

The most important things to know about me, is that I'm a hardworker, I'm ambitious and I'm comitted. I'm adventurous and I always try to think outside the box to find new and better ways to reach my goals.

The thing I love about code is the ability to make things. When I write words or numbers, something actually appears (if i write it correctly).
I love simple designs - less is more, but I also love colors. It's a fine line between simple and boring and a design should NEVER be boring.
